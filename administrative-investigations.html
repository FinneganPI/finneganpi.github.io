Independent investigations for employers, agencies, and counsel

QED Investigations provides independent administrative and workplace investigations for public agencies, private employers, and legal counsel throughout California’s Central Valley. Investigations are conducted objectively and documented with clear findings based on evidence, witness statements, and applicable policy.

Common matters addressed

  • Employee misconduct and policy violations
  • Harassment, discrimination, and hostile work environment complaints
  • Workplace violence threats and safety-related incidents
  • Dishonesty, fraud, time theft, and property theft allegations
  • Alcohol/drug impairment and fitness-for-duty concerns
  • Critical incident / administrative reviews (as appropriate to scope)

Process and deliverables

  • Intake and scope definition (objectives, policy framework, stakeholders)
  • Interview planning and witness sequencing
  • Interviews, evidence collection, and documentation
  • Findings supported by facts and corroboration where available
  • Deliverables by request: written report, executive summary, or briefing

Service area

Fresno County, Madera County, Merced County, Mariposa County, and surrounding Central Valley jurisdictions.
